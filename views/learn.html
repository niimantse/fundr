Fundr Africa

We are Africa's leading crowd-funding platform focused on social projects. Using our platform, people can raise funds to support causes which we both care about. We believe that it doesn't take much to make a difference, because with little drops of water we can build a mighty ocean.

Values we share

Our core values lies in Generosity, Passion and Excellence. Each of these go into each and every single project which we back here on Fundr Africa, and that's why we do continue to recieve the support of various persons across the continent

Difference that you can make

As part of our donors here at Fundr Africa, you will be a part of making a big difference. Our donors get the opportunity of not only supporting projects during the funding period but also being part of the execution phase of each and every single project
